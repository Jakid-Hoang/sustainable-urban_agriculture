SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

SDG 11 aims to make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. This includes ensuring access to adequate housing, improving public transportation, reducing urban pollution, and creating green spaces for a better quality of life in cities.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

SDG 12 aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns. This involves reducing food waste, promoting sustainable business practices, and encouraging individuals to make environmentally conscious choices.

SDG 13: Climate Action

SDG 13 calls for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. This includes reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting renewable energy, and supporting climate adaptation strategies to build resilience.
